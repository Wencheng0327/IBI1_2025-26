question:  What was the distribution of DALYs across all countries in 2019?

The code for this question is on lines 62 to 74 in dalys.py.

Discussion:
The histogram shows that the distribution of DALYs across countries in 2019 was right-skewed. 
Most countries were concentrated in the lower to middle range, roughly between 18,000 and 35,000 DALYs. 
As DALYs increased, the number of countries generally decreased. 
There was also a long right tail, with a small number of countries showing much higher DALYs than most others. 
This means that although many countries had relatively moderate DALYs, a few countries had a much heavier disease burden.
